About Us

About Us

Mission

Asian Advocates is a student led organization that aiams to give a voice to the Asian community and shine light on all the culture & achievements the community has brought forth into this world while also educating the public about all the racism that the Asian community has faced both in the past and in the present. We do that through our inform, respond, & empower motto. Making sure that our community is not ignored or forgotten about.

Motto

  • Inform -- We inform the public about both past and current events regarding the Asian community -- talking about historic figures, hate crimes, xenophobia, and many more.
  • Respond -- We respond to the news and any injustice we see against the Asian community.
  • Empower -- We empower the Asian community to stand up and advocate for our community, because our voices need to be heard.
